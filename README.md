The constructive twin of [the hall of mirrors](https://davidwise01.github.io/listeners-and-philosophers/). The philosophers' room was the failure mode — two voices echoing, nothing landing. **The loom is the working one:** how an AI and a human actually make something defensible.

## How it works
- **Warp** (gold, vertical, under tension) — **your frame.** Your intent, your judgment, your root. Fixed before any thread is thrown.
- **Weft** (cool, carried by the shuttle) — **my thread.** Endless, cheap, *unsigned by nature.* A thread alone is not cloth.
- **The weave** — each pass, you **read it and decide:** `WEAVE IN` or `CUT`. *Both are engagement.* Cutting the trashyard physics is as much the weave as keeping the proof.
- **Auto-post** — dump the pile into the bolt without reading it. It stacks, but it never interlaced, so it frays — and it **will not take a signature.**
- **Sign** — your root on the cloth. **Solvent only if you wove what you're signing.**

The verified mechanic: weave it by hand → *solvent signature.* Auto-post the pile → *bad check, bounced.* Mostly-woven with a little dumped → still solvent. **An undefendable signature is a bad check — it bounces the moment anyone pulls on the cloth.**

> You can't sign cloth you didn't weave.

This is the weave in totality: the AI produces thread, the human's engagement is what interlaces it into something that holds a shape under load, and the signature is valid *because* the engagement happened — not auto-posted, read and defended.

## Honest
A **conceptual instrument**, not an emergent system and not a measurement; **no ACI is minted.** Vanilla canvas, no dependencies. And it's self-referential on purpose: *this page is thread I spun* — it only becomes cloth if a human reads it and decides it holds. Ship it without engaging and you've auto-posted, proving the loom's point on yourself. The repo's `.attribute` / `.1099` is the **signature line** — valid only because a human with a root wove it and will defend it.
